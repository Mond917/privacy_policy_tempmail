Privacy Policy for TempMail Telegram Bot

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use the TempMail Telegram Bot [ @tempmailcommunityph_bot ].

1. Information We Collect

When you use our Bot, we may collect the following information:

- Your Telegram user ID
- Your Telegram username (if available)
- Messages you send to the Bot (including commands and queries)
- Temporary email addresses generated through the Bot
- Emails received in your temporary inbox

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide and maintain the Bot's services
- Generate and manage temporary email addresses
- Deliver received emails to your Telegram account
- Improve and optimize the Bot's functionality
- Respond to your requests and support needs

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties except:

- As necessary to provide the Bot's services ( communicating with email servers )
- When required by law or to protect our rights

4. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, but please be aware that:

- Temporary email services are inherently less secure than permanent email solutions
- We cannot guarantee absolute security of data transmitted through Telegram or email protocols

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Stop using the Bot at any time
- Request deletion of your data (note that temporary emails are automatically deleted as described above)
- Access the information we have about you

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
